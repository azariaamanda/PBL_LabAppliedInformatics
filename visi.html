VISION AND MISSION

OUR VISION

To become a leading laboratory in the development and application of innovative information technology that facilitates the three
pillars of education, research, and teaching, supports sustainable digital transformation, encourages cross-sector collaboration, and
creates data-driven smart solutions to address the challenges of Industry 4.0.

OUR MISION

1

Information Technology Application

Applying algorithms, data processing, and distributed systems for innovative technological solutions to improve productivity and information accessibility.

2

Technological Innovation for Industry 4.0 Solution

Focusing on Industry 4.0 solutions through innovative automation technologies, system integration, and efficient real-time data processing.

3

Quad-Helix Collaboration

Maintaining the relevance of Industry 4.0 through quad-helix synergy, fostering innovation, delivering effective solutions, and driving sustainable digital transformation.

4

Distributed Data-Based System

Integrating distributed data with blockchain, big data, and machine learning improves the security, integrity, accessibility, and reliability of information.
